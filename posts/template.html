Writing Your Post

To create a new blog post:

  1. Copy this template file
  2. Rename it to match your post slug (e.g., my-post-title.html)
  3. Replace POST_TITLE with your post title
  4. Replace POST_DATE with the publication date
  5. Write your content in the post-content div
  6. Add the post to the blogPosts array in script.js

LaTeX Examples

You can use standard LaTeX syntax:

  • Inline: $x^2 + y^2 = r^2$ renders as $x^2 + y^2 = r^2$
  • Display: $$\sum_{i=1}^{n} i = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}$$ renders as a centered equation
